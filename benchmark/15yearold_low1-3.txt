FrankPosted 28 October 19:21
Hello Ivana,
I am a veterinarian, specializing in birds. It is okay to give injured chickens aspirin if they are showing signs that they are in pain. When prescribing aspirin to birds, I follow the guidelines published in Clinical Avian Medicine. Chickens should receive 5mg of aspirin per kg of body weight. You can give this 3-4 times per day until you can see your veterinarian. It is very important to follow up with your vet. Good luck!
