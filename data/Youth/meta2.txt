Our cookies on other apps and websites

We use cookies on apps and websites owned by other companies that use Meta technologies. These cookies help other companies share information with us about your activity on their apps and websites.

When you visit these apps and websites, we use the information that we receive to:

* Personalise your experience on Meta Products and to show you ads, if we show you ads.
* Provide services to you off Instagram and Facebook, such as the ability to log in to other apps and websites using Facebook.
* Improve Meta Products.
* Help companies who use our tools to analyse and measure their ad performance.
